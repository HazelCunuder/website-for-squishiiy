Who am I?

Heya!

My name is Cloie, better known as Squishii.y. Since I was little, I have always enjoyed drawing and creating things. Of course, like most people, my first artworks were terrible. But after years of practice, I finally reached a level where I felt comfortable sharing my creation with you. However, this doesn't mean that the grind is over, I am still striving to improve every single day of my life. I really hope that you'll enjoy my work, and if you want to help and support me, feel free to look up my shop. Thank you very much!
